WEBSITE DESIGN FOR MEATH’S TOP GOLFING DESTINATION HEADFORT GOLF CLUB

About the project

Headfort Golf Club are one of Meath and Ireland’s top golf courses placing highly in national rankings. While working at Flo Web Design we were brought in to redesign their current site which was outdated in style and lacking a user friendly CMS. This project was a collaborative one between everyone in our office. My main roles were creative direction, visual design, UX, front end development and CMS integration.
