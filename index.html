A little bit about my beginnings

I started out as a graphic designer by creating artwork for clubnights that I began running when I was 17. I went on to study multimedia & web development for a year in Cavan Institute where I got a work placement in a small web design studio called Flo Web Design. I spent 3 years here learning how to design and develop websites, applications and social media campaigns for clients like Iceland, Killeen Castle, X-tra Vision and many more.

After 3 years in Flo Web, I moved on to a digital agency called Ebow for a contract role and worked on some big projects with GAA, Disney, Maxol, Orange and DNA.

Later that year I started a new role as a sole UI designer within Intel Education. I lead the design for the department on some large scale products which include websites, web apps and native apps. I also assist in front-end development and QA when needed.

So, what kind of stuff do I do

Design Strategy

All good designs start with a planning process. Researching with real users and brainstorming with stakeholders is the first step.

Visual Design

Once the planning has been finalised it's time to design. I sketch, wireframe and prototype before moving to full visual designs.

Front-End Development

Designers who can code their designs have an advantage over those who don't. I work with HTML, CSS, SASS and Wordpress.
